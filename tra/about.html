About us

We’re a small team based in Tallinn, Estonia. We just decided to start a small idea that would help people find a fitting job more effectively and easily without having to go through too many pointless forms.

Problem

Unemployment has always been a big issue in the world, people can’t support either themselves or their families. Poverty is a problem that has been with us for the longest time, and we’re here to try and fix it.

Solution

Fob - the app where we can gain experience, get paid to do it and eventually - find our dream jobs
