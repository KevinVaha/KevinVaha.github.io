Mone is an app that helps people understand where their money is going
and thus can help budget their expenses

Instead on having to put your expenses and incomes in manually, Mone can do that for you
by looking at your bank transactions.
Mone does not need anything other than your transactions.

transactions

See all incoming and outgoing transactions. Set a custom timeframe or see all of your transactions, helping you better understand where the money is going.

Expenses

Mone looks at all your transactions and divides them into categories. There will be a visual representation of your expenses.

Dashboard view

Mone generates a dashboard view for you so you can get an overview of recent transactions and expenses quickly.
